Jellyfish Biomechanics and Hydrodynamics


Biomechanics and hydrodynamics of Aurelia aurita

Jellyfish are believed to be the most energy efficient swimmers on Earth. 99% of their body mass is also made up of seawater. I am interested in how jellyfish are able to achieve such efficient swimming, and how different environmental conditions can affect their biomechanical and swimming performance.
