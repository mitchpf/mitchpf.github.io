Jellyfish Biomechanics and Hydrodynamics


Biomechanics and hydrodynamics of Aurelia aurita

Free-swimming jellyfish ("medusae") are believed to be the most energetically efficient swimmers on Earth, and they are spread throughout all oceans and depths. I am interested in the biomechanics and fluid dynamics of jellyfish swimming and how they are affected by environmental conditions – such as temperature and hydrostatic pressure – that differ across time and place.
